Midpoint Calculator

This is the midpoint calculator. You can use this to test out hypothetical situations. Fill out at least two of the boxes, click the “calculate” button, and then gather the information in order to solve for any hypothetical demands, offers, or midpoints.

Offer/Demand Tracker

This tool allows you to track offers and demands, the respective midpoint, and the differential between offers. You can also email your history to yourself. Fill in the demand and the offer and it will calculate the midpoint and differential between multiple offers and keep track of the negotiation data.
